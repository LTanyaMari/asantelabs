About Us

àSanté Story

Our cosmetic chemist has over thirty years of experience in the industry, having served as the lead chemist for a number of manufacturers where he conducted research and prepared formulations as the basis for several hair and skin care products currently on the market.

Two decades ago, àSanté Labs evolved as an independent manufacturer producing quality all-natural hair and skin care products under private label contracts for professional use by cosmetologists, hair stylists, estheticians, and massage therapists.

The àSanté line has included shampoos, hair and skin oils, massage oils, relaxers, gels, and conditioners. In response to growing customer demand, àSanté Oil was developed as our flagship product. It is a light, multi-purpose all-natural hair and skin care oil of uncompromising quality.
